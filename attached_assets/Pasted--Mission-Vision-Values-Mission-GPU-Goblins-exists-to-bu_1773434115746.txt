## Mission, Vision & Values --- # Mission GPU Goblins exists to build a global community that raises awareness about the growing centralization of compute power while using culture, memes, and storytelling to inspire curiosity about the infrastructure that powers the internet. Through the GPU Goblins universe, the project transforms complex topics like data centers, artificial intelligence infrastructure, and GPU monopolization into a decentralized cultural movement driven by creativity, transparency, and community participation. At its core, GPU Goblins aims to make one idea impossible to ignore: Compute power should not belong to a handful of corporations. --- # Vision Our vision is to create a community-driven ecosystem that blends internet culture with real conversations about the future of computing infrastructure. As artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and digital economies expand, access to GPUs and large-scale compute resources is becoming one of the defining power structures of the modern internet. GPU Goblins imagines a future where: - communities are aware of how compute infrastructure shapes the digital world - centralized compute monopolies are questioned and challenged - culture and technology intersect to create new forms of decentralized movements The long-term goal is not simply to build a token or a meme project. It is to grow a cultural narrative that encourages people to think critically about who controls the computational resources shaping the future. --- # Values ### Curiosity GPU Goblins encourages exploration and questioning. Understanding the systems behind the internet begins with curiosity about how they work. --- ### Transparency All core infrastructure, token mechanics, and project development aim to remain transparent and publicly verifiable. The Vaults may be hidden in the lore, but the project itself is not. --- ### Community Ownership GPU Goblins is built around the belief that communities are more powerful than centralized institutions. The story, culture, and growth of the Goblin network are shaped by its members. --- ### Creativity Memes, art, storytelling, and community contributions are essential to the GPU Goblins ecosystem. The Goblin universe expands through the imagination of its community. --- ### Awareness The project encourages discussion about real-world technological trends including: - data center expansion - AI infrastructure growth - GPU supply concentration - the power dynamics of compute ownership These conversations are the foundation of the Goblin narrative. --- # The Goblin Ethos Within the world of GPU Goblins, every member of the community follows three simple principles: No compute shall go to waste. Idle cycles are opportunity. The truth belongs to the network. Information must flow. No Goblin hoards alone. Power belongs to the community. --- # Closing Statement GPU Goblins is not simply a cryptocurrency project. It is a cultural experiment exploring the intersection of technology, infrastructure, and internet communities. As the digital world becomes increasingly dependent on compute power, the Goblins exist to remind the world that behind every server rack, data center, and GPU cluster lies a deeper question: Who controls the compute? And perhaps more importantly: Who should?
